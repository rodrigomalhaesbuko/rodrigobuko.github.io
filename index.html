Inn Hell

Inn Hell is a digital game, for iOS, in which the protagonist, with the help of the Devil, must smuggle beer into the skies in exchange for his spirit being free from eternal torture.

The game brings together two genres, roguelike and tactics and aims to evade the casual theme of mobile games, seeking more difficult mechanics and with a high learning curve.

I developed InnHell during my time at Apple Developer Academy. I learned a lot from making all the game structure from zero and creating my own Random Map Generator algorithm, creating procedural levels fitting the Roguelike genre.

O Formigueiro

O Formigueiro ("The anthill") is a stealth and survival game about a half-robot ant inside an anthill full of monstrous robot ants.

Your objective is to get out of this huge maze all in the dark, but don't worry because you got an ant sonar. Your powerful sonar will make you see through all the enemies and puzzles and help you see the light of the day out of the anthill.

O Formigueiro was developed by Prisma Game Lab an independent game studio made by students from Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro. I was part of the Unity Developers team, I worked on the sonar effect and all other graphic features about the game, developing some levels and implementing the core mechanics.

Paintings Don't Move

In Paintings Don't Move, you fight inside a cursed ancient Egyptian painting to keep enemies from reaching the pillar that is the source of your power. At the same time, you must keep the museum visitor from noticing your painting is moving! To both those ends, you must use your power to freeze time!

This game was developed during the "Mini Jam 61: Ancient Egypt" hosted by Itch.io. It was a very interesting craft and develop the whole game concept in only 72 hours. I learn about basic shader properties, make a realistic and satisfactory scope, and how to coordinate a team through the entire jam.

For more info check out the game page

Throw Arms

Throw Hands is a Local/Online Multiplayer fight game with a funny twist: whenever your character punches, it launches its arm. To punch again, you must pick the arm back up.

This game was developed in Unity and used the free Photon Bolt asset to implement all the multiplayer experience. I worked mainly on the multiplayer features, gameplay, and bug fixes.
